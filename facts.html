*Arnold Schwarzenegger’s famous debut line “I’ll be back” was originally scripted as “I’ll come back.”

One afternoon during a break in filming, Arnold Schwarzenegger went into a restaurant in downtown L.A. to get some lunch. He realized all too late that he was still in Terminator makeup with a missing eye, exposed jawbone and burned flesh.
